SEVENTEEN

13 Members + 3 Sub-Units + 1 Team = 17

Seventeen is a Korean boy band consisting of 13 members. They debuted on May 26, 2015 under Pledis Entertainment. The group is split into 3 Sub-Units: Hip-Hop, Performance, and Vocal Team. Their official fandom name is Carat and their official fan colors are “Rose Quartz” and “Serenity.”
